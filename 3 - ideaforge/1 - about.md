# IdeaForge — AI-Powered Business Validation Platform

> **Website:** [https://ideaforge.id](https://ideaforge.id)
> **Platform:** Web Application (SaaS)
> **Tech Stack:** React + Supabase
> **Users:** 48+ active users, 51+ business ideas validated
> **Tagline:** *Ubah Ide Bisnis Menjadi Strategi Nyata dengan AI*

---

## Product Overview

**IdeaForge** is an AI-powered platform that provides **professional business validation** using proven business frameworks for Indonesian entrepreneurs — instantly, affordably, and accessibly.

The platform enables aspiring founders, startup teams, and business students to validate their business ideas in minutes instead of weeks by leveraging AI analysis based on established frameworks such as **Lean Startup**, **SWOT Analysis**, and **Blue Ocean Strategy**.

---

## Problem Statement

Indonesian entrepreneurs face significant barriers when validating business ideas:

| Challenge | Traditional Approach | Impact |
|---|---|---|
| **Cost** | Business consultants charge Rp 4-15 million | Most early-stage founders can't afford professional validation |
| **Speed** | Consulting takes 2-4 weeks | Slow feedback cycles delay market entry |
| **Availability** | Limited by consultant schedules | Founders wait days for appointments |
| **Consistency** | Quality varies by consultant | No standardized analysis framework |
| **Accessibility** | Concentrated in large cities | Rural/suburban entrepreneurs are underserved |

IdeaForge eliminates these barriers by providing **AI-driven analysis at 1% of the cost**, available **24/7**, with **consistent framework-based output**.

## Core Features

### 1. Business Validation
Deep analysis of **market size**, **target audience**, and **competitive landscape** — powered by AI with structured business frameworks.

### 2. Lean Canvas Generation
Automatically generated **one-page business plan** following the Lean Canvas model: Problem, Solution, Key Metrics, Unfair Advantage, Channels, Customer Segments, Cost Structure, Revenue Streams.

### 3. MVP Recommendations
Clear, actionable guidance on building a **Minimum Viable Product** — what to build first, feature prioritization, and development approach.

### 4. Go-to-Market Strategy
Specific strategies for **market entry** and **initial user acquisition** — channels, messaging, launch tactics.

### 5. Financial Planning
Projections for **revenue**, **operational costs**, and **Break-Even Point (BEP)** — helping founders understand financial viability.

### 6. Report Export
Professional documents (**PDF** and **PowerPoint/PPTX**) ready to be presented to partners, investors, or stakeholders.

## Product Background

IdeaForge was built to **democratize business validation** for Indonesian entrepreneurs who traditionally had to pay millions of rupiah for professional consulting. By combining AI technology with proven business frameworks (Lean Startup, SWOT, Blue Ocean Strategy), the platform converts unstructured business ideas into professional, structured validation reports in seconds.

The product demonstrates competencies in **AI product development**, **SaaS platform architecture**, **subscription-based business modeling**, and **end-to-end product lifecycle management**.
