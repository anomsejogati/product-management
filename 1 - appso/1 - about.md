## Product Overview

**APPSO** (Aplikasi Sekolah Online) is a SaaS-based **School Information Management System** (Sistem Informasi Manajemen Sekolah) designed to digitalize school operations for all education levels in Indonesia — from SD/MI, SMP/MTs, to SMA/SMK/MA.

The platform streamlines school administration through integrated modules covering **finance management**, **academic operations**, **digital report cards**, **online exams**, **learning management (LMS)**, and **parent engagement** — all accessible 24/7 from any device connected to the internet.

### Vision

Membantu lembaga pendidikan di Indonesia dalam meningkatkan kualitas layanan pendidikan berbasis teknologi.

*(Helping educational institutions in Indonesia improve the quality of technology-based educational services.)*

## Core Modules

### 1. Finance Module (Modul Keuangan)
- **Pembayaran SPP & Biaya Lainnya** — School fee payments (tuition, registration, etc.) with WhatsApp notification to parents
- **Tabungan Siswa** — Student savings management with automatic balance calculation, integrated with the payment system
- **Buku Kas Sekolah** — School cash book for income sources and expense categorization

### 2. Academic Module (Modul Akademik)
- **Penjadwalan & Absensi** — Class scheduling and student attendance tracking
- **Jurnal Guru & RPP** — Teacher journals and lesson planning
- **Penilaian & Raport Digital** — Grade input and digital report card generation supporting **K13 and Merdeka Curriculum**
- **Angka Kredit Siswa** — Student merit/demerit point tracking

### 3. Learning Module (Modul Pembelajaran)
- **Learning Management System (LMS)** — Online learning content delivery
- **Ujian Online** — Online examination platform

### 4. Counseling Module (Modul BK)
- **Bimbingan Konseling** — Student counseling records for BK teachers and homeroom teachers

### 5. Parent Module (Modul Orang Tua)
- **Android App** (Google Play) — View academic reports, tuition billing, savings, report cards, attendance, and announcements
- **Email & WhatsApp Notifications** — Real-time payment transaction alerts

### 6. School Website & PPDB Online
- **Website Sekolah** — Indexed by Google for school visibility
- **PPDB Online** — Online student admission registration

## Product Background

APPSO was created in **2020** as a personal product initiative during the founder's Master of Information Technology program at **Universitas Indonesia**. The product was built from scratch — from ideation, business model analysis, MVP development, market launch, to iteration — following full **Product Development Lifecycle (PDLC)** principles.

The product has since grown to serve **150+ schools** across multiple provinces in Indonesia, including West Java, Central Java, East Java, Bengkulu, and Lampung.
